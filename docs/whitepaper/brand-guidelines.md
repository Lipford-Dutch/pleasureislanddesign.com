# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

Pleasure Island Design positions itself as a **premium, coastal luxury** brand that makes high-end design accessible. The aesthetic is refined, warm, and professional — reflecting the beautiful SE North Carolina coastal lifestyle.

## Logo

The primary logo features:
- Elegant serif-style wordmark: "pleasure island design"
- Tagline: "INTERIORS • CABINETRY • FURNITURE"
- Icon: A chair and lamp in sage green
- The dot over the "i" in "design" is a leaf/plant motif

The circular badge watermark features:
- Deep navy circle background
- Lighthouse/coastal house icon
- "PLEASURE ISLAND" on top arc
- "DESIGN" on bottom arc
- Wave motif at base

## Tone of Voice

- **Warm and confident** — not corporate, not casual
- **Expert without being jargon-heavy** — clients trust, but aren't confused
- **Local and community-focused** — Pleasure Island pride
- **Aspirational but accessible** — luxury results, reasonable investment

## Brand Do's

- ✅ Use sage green for primary CTAs
- ✅ Use Playfair Display for all section headings
- ✅ Show real before/after photos prominently
- ✅ Lead with craftsmanship and quality
- ✅ Reference local neighborhoods and areas

## Brand Don'ts

- ❌ Do not use competing orange accent (previous site color)
- ❌ Do not use condensed or display fonts for body copy
- ❌ Do not use stock kitchen photos as primary showcase
- ❌ Do not use "cheap" or "budget" language
- ❌ Do not use pure black (#000) for text — use charcoal

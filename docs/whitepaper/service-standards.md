# Service Standards

## Quality Promise

Every Pleasure Island Design project is delivered to the following standards:

- **Timeline:** 5-8 business days from start to completion
- **Preparation:** Full surface prep before any finish is applied
- **Materials:** Professional-grade stains, paints, and topcoats only
- **Cleanup:** Site left clean at end of every day
- **Communication:** Daily progress updates to client
- **Satisfaction:** Work not complete until client approves

## Service Catalog

### Cabinet Refinishing (Core Service)
Transform the look and feel of existing cabinets with professional staining or painting.
- Full surface preparation (cleaning, sanding, de-glossing)
- Color consultation included
- Multiple finish options: matte, satin, semi-gloss
- Topcoat for durability

### Cabinet Repair
Restore damaged or worn cabinet components.
- Hinge replacement
- Door realignment
- Structural repairs
- Veneer repair

### Hardware Installation
Update pulls, handles, and hinges for a fresh modern look.
- Wide selection of hardware styles
- Precision drilling for consistent placement
- Old hardware removal and disposal

### Cabinet Refacing
Replace cabinet door and drawer fronts while keeping existing boxes.
- More affordable than full replacement
- Wide range of styles and materials
- Includes new hardware

### Drawer Upgrades
Improve kitchen functionality with upgraded drawer systems.
- Soft-close mechanisms
- Drawer organizers and dividers
- Custom sizing options

### Design Consultation
Expert guidance for your cabinet and kitchen refresh project.
- Style and color recommendations
- Hardware selection assistance
- Before/after planning

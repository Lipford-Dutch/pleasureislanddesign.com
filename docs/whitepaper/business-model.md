# Business Model

## Revenue Model

Pleasure Island Design operates as a **project-based service business**:

- Revenue is generated per project (not subscription or retainer)
- Projects range from single-room cabinet refreshes to full kitchen transformations
- Upsell opportunities: hardware, drawer upgrades, design consultation

## Service Area

**Primary:** Wilmington NC and immediate surroundings
**Secondary:** SE North Carolina coast (Kure Beach, Carolina Beach, Wrightsville Beach, Leland, Southport)

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Speed** — 5-8 day turnaround vs. weeks for full renovation
2. **Price** — Fraction of cabinet replacement cost
3. **Quality** — Professional-grade materials and techniques
4. **Trust** — Award-winning, community-recognized brand
5. **Local** — Deep roots in Pleasure Island community

## Growth Strategy

- **Organic referrals** — Primary acquisition channel (satisfied clients refer friends)
- **Nextdoor** — Strong community presence, award-winning presence
- **Digital** — Website, Google search, social media (expanding)
- **Phase 3 expansion** — Blog content, video marketing, paid ads, review strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

- Priced as premium service, not commodity
- Transparent estimates before work begins
- No hidden fees
- Value communicated through before/after photography and testimonials

## Customer Journey

1. Discovery (Google, referral, social media)
2. Website visit → Review portfolio + testimonials
3. Contact via form, phone, or scheduling link
4. Free consultation / estimate
5. Project scheduling (5-8 days)
6. Project completion + client approval
7. Follow-up + review request
8. Referral loop
